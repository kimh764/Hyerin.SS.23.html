About

The Plant Sharing App is a platform for plant enthusiasts to share and communicate about their plants. This app focuses on fostering a community where users can share the joy and experiences of plant cultivation. Users can document their plant collections, explore others' plants, and exchange knowledge.

Journal

Keyword

Users can register their plants on the app, providing basic information and regularly updating details such as growth records, watering schedules, and any specific notes to track the health of their plants.

User

Many people believe that cultivating plants is beneficial for their health, providing mental relaxation and stability. Additionally, there is a growing awareness that plants contribute to purifying the air and enhancing the environment, attracting individuals who aspire to lead a healthier lifestyle. With the increasing interest in plants, there has also been a rise in the number of users of plant-related apps.
